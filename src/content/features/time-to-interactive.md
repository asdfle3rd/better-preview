Every navigation measures the underlying Time-to-Interactive (TTI) of your Page. No more need to get into clunky Lighthouse UIs to get exact numbers on how fast your content loads. The previews are cached load quickly, so you can start interacting with your content testing links, buttons, and animations in an instant instantly.
